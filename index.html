Services

How can I help you?

Do you want to take your business to the next level or start your project you have in mind for so long?

Apps, Landing Pages, Websites and Blogs are part of my daily work routine. Below you will find my design tools to help you bring to life what you have in mind.

User Experience Design

Know your project. Before anything, I will analyze your business with UX tools that will give me inputs and important feedback of your business health or project idea. This stage includes User Research tools (adapted to the size and needs of your business) that may include competitor analysis, online inquiries, personas, user stories and customer experience maps. After that we can proceed to moodboards, information architecture, low and high fidelity wireframes and user testing.

Deliveries: pdf presentation.

User Interface Design

Let’s go Visual. After the UX part and based on the developed wireframes, I will work the Visual Design of your project with Sketch. At the end you will get a pixel perfect design for your app, landing page or website.

Deliveries: Sketch file and all screens exported to jpg.

Prototyping

Interactions going real. At this stage you will be able to check and test your product with some interactions that will allow you to preview the end result of your project. The prototyping will be done with Invision.

Deliveries: Invision link.

Animation

Infographics and Banners. Animations like Infographics, banners or animated and interactive catalogs can also be a valuable resource for your project. I will do the storyboard previously using Sketch and after your approval I start animating with Animate CC.

Deliveries: zip file with the .html, .js and images.
